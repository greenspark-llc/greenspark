How much quieter is electric equipment compared to their gas counterparts? ▼

In terms of sound, gas mowers are comparable to the sound of motorcycles, meanwhile electric mowers are comparable to the sound of a dryer. 75db electric mower vs 100db gas mower. 25db can be a significant difference as every 6db doubles the perceived loudness. 100db is about 4x louder than 75db.

Do electric mowers perform like gas mowers? ▼

Yes! Our top-of-the-line commercial-grade electric equipment ensures that you get the results you want without the fumes and fuss.
